What It Is

This is a website that I built for Derek Riley, freelance photographer and also my husband. It includes a Home page, an About page, a Gallery page, and a Pricing page. It is a professional website to help attract customers, allow them to see his work, and allow them to book a session easily.

Key Home Page Features:

  • Call to action at the top
  • Some information about where Sundust Photography is located, as well as traveling distance
  • A "Book Now" button, which opens up an email draft addressed to sundustphotography@gmail.com
  • A changing image in the center, which loops through 5 images.

Key About Page Features:

  • A few paragraphs about who he is and why he does photography
  • Contact information at the bottom of the screen
